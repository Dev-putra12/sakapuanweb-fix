#BerhatiBeraniBerdikari

As a Balinese women's self-empowerment community, Sakapuan exists with dedication and determination to develop the character and careers of young Balinese women. Through Sakapuan's vision, namely creating 1000 Balinese women who have empathy and independence, we strive to support and maximize the potential of our women in facing gender construction and the challenges of Balinese patriarchy. Therefore, Sakapuan invites all of us, without exception, to be part of this struggle by carrying out existing missions to make Balinese women #BerhatiBeraniBerdikari people.

OUR MISSION

Spread awareness, information and education about the existence of patriarchal problems in Bali and the importance of women's empowerment.

Facilitate women's character and career development.

Maximizing Sakapuan's internal and external resources.

OUR VISSION

Calling 1000 Balinese women to have empathy and independence.

FEATURED PROGRAMS

SAKAPUAN TALKS

This is a place where Balinese women's voices truly matter and become even louder. We'll gather, listen, and discuss everything related to women's issues, including Balinese gender constructions.

SAKAPUAN COMMUNITY EMPOWERMENT

A place for Balinese women to learn, develop and grow. By implementing the concept of "Being Brave and Independent", SCE is committed to creating a platform that can support the empowerment of Balinese women in terms of leadership.

CAREER DAY

is an activity in the form of a talk show that discusses matters related to business design and creation (UMKM). Sakapuan will present Balinese female speakers who have pioneered MSMEs to share information about the careers of the younger generation in MSMEs, which includes designing, managing and marketing MSME businesses. This activity will also invite MSME owners in the Bali region so that a better forum for discussion can be created.
